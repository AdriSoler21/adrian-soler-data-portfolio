# Customer Revenue Risk Prediction

Portfolio-safe version of a customer analytics project focused on identifying customers at risk of revenue decline.

## What it demonstrates

- Customer-level feature engineering
- Recency, frequency and revenue metrics
- 12-month, 6-month and 3-month behavioural windows
- Machine Learning classification with Random Forest
- Business-oriented model interpretation through feature importance

## Privacy note

No proprietary data, company names, internal paths, credentials or confidential identifiers are included.
